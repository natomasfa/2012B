Academy Age Group: U9 - U12 

Why fundamentals are important

Only the very best players will pass the ball immediately once or twice a game when they receive it. You have to learn to play yourself clear-- otherwise you will lose possession or will be forced to pass the ball on to a teammate who's in just as difficult a position. Or it becomes a kick and run soccer that depends on luck which is no good either. That's why basic ball handling techniques are so important.

List of Skill Moves


  • Lunge feint. Pre-fake when possible, shift weight away from desired direction and land in a lunge, drop should on same side as plant foot, accelerate opposite direction. of the lunger. Watch Video!
  • Step Over. Approach the ball making it look like you’re going to make a pass with the inside of your foot. Instead of actually making contact with the ball, your foot should move around the ball. Using the outside of the foot you used to move around the ball, dribble in the opposite direction. Watch Video!
  • Cruyff. Pre-fake when possible, plant foot in front of ball, swing leg back hard as if you are striking the ball, do not strike but tuck ball behind plant foot using inside of laces, next touch should be outside of plant foot to change directions, keep ball close, go with speed into and out of the skill move. Try it now!
  • L-turns. Drag back ball with sole of foot beyond plant foot heel and them behind the plant foot heel, use of outside of plant foot to go other direction. Watch Video!
