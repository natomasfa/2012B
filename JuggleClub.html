Juggle Challenge

The Natomas FA Juggling Club is open to all players within the Natomas FA Organization. Compete to be the best juggler within Natomas FA and get your record added to our leaderboards.
Also please feel encouraged to send your videos showing your best tricks. Are you the best at tricks? Then show us! We want to see some magic!

Rules

- Player's Coach must observe the attempt and keep count
- The ball may start in player's hands
- Only touches with the foot will be counted
- A player may achieve the bronze, silver, gold and platinum only once a season

Levels

Bronze

10 Keep ups/Juggles

Silver

20 Keep ups/Juggles

Gold

50 Keep ups/Juggles

Platinum

100+ Keep ups/Juggles
