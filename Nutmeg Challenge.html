Nutmeg Challenge

The Natomas FA Nutmeg Challenge is season long challenge for the Natomas FA players.   

Rules

- Player will play ball through the legs of an opponent and collect it on the other side.
- The ball must be collected on the other side or the nutmeg does not count
- Player can nutmeg other player or coach 
- Player with most coach nutmegs will earn PRO award at the end of the season
- Player with most player nutmegs will earn ELITE award at the end of the season
